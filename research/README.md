# Research index

This directory contains the evidence behind Levera's product and go-to-market hypotheses.

## Main research questions

1. Do parents experience a strong problem around building independence and life skills?
2. Why do families stop using chore and reward applications?
3. Which mechanics improve or damage child retention?
4. Are parents willing to pay for progress visibility and structured guidance?
5. Which competitors and distribution channels matter most?

## Directories

- `competitors/` — Greenlight and BusyKid source reports.
- `market/` — focused research on pain, churn, retention and willingness to pay.
- `audience/` — parenting accounts, channels, creators and content examples.
- `raw/` — unprocessed source exports retained for traceability.

## Research quality notes

- The FamilyHero export was incomplete and moved to `archive/low-quality/`.
- Exact duplicate exports were removed.
- Market claims and statistics must be verified against primary sources before public, legal, press, store or investor use.
- Secondary research does not replace Mom Test interviews or the W4 pilot.
